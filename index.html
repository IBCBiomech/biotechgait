What is BioTechGait

BioTechGait is a software application that register motion data from pressure insoles and sensors. Data is gathered to analyze it by high quality AI algorithms. Giving precise predictions in diagnostics reports about human gait.

This product has been created by IBC Bio ™ for customers who want to perform an effective gait study without expensive laboratories. It uses high quality medical sensors created by IEE Actisense System ™.

Features

HQ Medical Weareables

BioTechGait is a product that uses
high quality medical wearables
based on IEE's Smart Foot product with ActiSense Inertial Sensors. Enabling lossless capture of motion data. This data is displayed in our fully native Desktop application based on the most established technologies for UI developments.

Analisys with AI and Reporting

Data analysis through easy and accurate editing of graphs to extract the necessary information. Execution of normalised GRF, Butterfly and Heatmap charts.
